# VOLTERRA — Art Direction

## Direction
Cinematic engineering / cyber-organic energy infrastructure.

## Visual metaphor
A single current travels through generation, storage, grid control and the city. Every interface element behaves like part of one electrical field.

## Palette
- Deep field: `#080C09`
- Acid current: `#C9FF43`
- Warm load: `#FFB84D`
- Cooling signal: `#70E6D2`
- Technical paper: `#EAF0DF`

## Motion system
Slow orbital motion, flowing dashed paths, responsive system states, sticky journey chapters and a horizontal operating-scale gallery.

## Signature interactions
- Energy pulse moving across the complete system
- Grid operating dashboard with switchable asset layers
- Live capacity impact model
- City illumination as the final state of the scroll journey
